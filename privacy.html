Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025 · Compliant with PDPA 2010

1. Introduction

STEVPAN ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 ("PDPA") of Malaysia. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following personal data when you place an order:

  • Name — to identify you and address your order
  • Phone number — for delivery coordination and order updates
  • Email — for order confirmation and payment receipt
  • Shipping address — for delivery of your order
  • Order notes — any special instructions you provide (optional)

We also collect technical data automatically when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, and device information, which are used solely for analytics and security purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Process and fulfill your order
  • Communicate order status and delivery updates
  • Process payments through our payment provider (CHIP)
  • Display your review on our public orders page (first name and city only)
  • Respond to your inquiries
  • Improve our website and services

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. We share data only with:

  • CHIP — payment processing (name, email, order details as required for transaction processing)
  • Courier services — your name, phone, and shipping address for delivery purposes only
  • Supabase — our database hosting provider, which stores data securely in compliance with international standards

5. Data on Public Pages

When you leave a review on our orders page, we display:

  • Your first name only (e.g., "Ahmad" not "Ahmad bin Ismail")
  • Your city/state only (e.g., "Kuala Lumpur" not your full address)
  • Your star rating and written comment
  • Your uploaded review photos

We deliberately never display your phone number, email, full name, or exact address on any public page.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase's cloud infrastructure with:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits by the hosting provider

Order data is retained indefinitely for order history and warranty purposes. You may request deletion of your review data at any time by contacting us.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for the website to function (session data)
  • LocalStorage — stores your pricing preferences locally in your browser; this data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or third parties

We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Your Rights Under PDPA

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correct — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Withdraw consent — request we stop processing your data (note: this may affect your ability to use our services)
  • Delete — request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@stevpan.com. We will respond within 21 business days as required by PDPA.

9. Data Breach

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected individuals as soon as reasonably practicable and report to the Personal Data Protection Commissioner (PDPC) where required under PDPA.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries:

  • Email: support@stevpan.com
  • WhatsApp: Available via the chat button on our website
