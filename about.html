What We Do

STEVPAN fabricates custom rectangular steel pizza pans from stainless steel. You choose the exact dimensions, thickness, corner radius, and ventilation hole pattern — our configurator shows you a live preview and instant pricing.

We use LASER cutting for precision cuts for corner radii, and ventilation holes per your layout. Every pan is individually made to order.
