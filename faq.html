Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Contact us on WhatsApp.

What material are the pans made from?

All our pans are made from stainless steel (304 FOOD grade). Stainless steel density is 7,850 kg/m³. They are not non-stick coated — they are raw stainless steel pans finishing with hairline texture designed for high-heat cooking.

How long does delivery take?

Production starts within 3-5 business days after payment. Typical total delivery time is 5-10 business days depending on your location and current order volume. Sabah/Sarawak orders may take slightly longer.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, but only before production starts. Once your order status changes to "In Production", cancellation is no longer possible since the pan is being custom-manufactured to your specifications. Please contact us immediately if you need to cancel.

What if the pan has defects?

If your pan arrives with manufacturing defects or doesn't match your order specifications, contact us within 7 days with photos. We'll arrange a free replacement. See our Return & Refund Policy for details.

Do you ship to Sabah and Sarawak?

Yes, we ship nationwide. Shipping rates for East Malaysia are higher due to courier costs. Exact rates are calculated based on your order weight and shown before checkout.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept FPX (online banking transfer) and credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) through CHIP, a Malaysian payment gateway approved by Bank Negara Malaysia.

Can I order multiple pans at once?

Currently, each order is for one pan configuration. If you need multiple pans, you can place separate orders. Contact us on WhatsApp if you need a bulk order — we may be able to offer a discount.

What size pan should I get?

The most popular size is 300×400mm at 5mm thickness, which fits standard home ovens. Our configurator shows the exact weight and price as you adjust dimensions. If unsure, check your oven's internal dimensions first.

Are ventilation holes necessary?

They're optional and free. Ventilation holes help with air circulation and steam release, which can improve crust quality. You can choose no holes, a center hole, a square pattern, or a ring pattern. All patterns are included at no extra cost.

How do I track my order?

After payment, you'll receive an Order ID. Go to our Track Order page, enter your Order ID, and you'll see real-time status updates from Pending → In Production → Shipping → Delivered.

Is the steel food-safe?

Stainless steel is widely used in commercial kitchens worldwide. New pans should be seasoned (heated with oil) before first use to create a natural non-stick surface and prevent rust. We do not apply any chemical coatings — it's pure stainless steel.
