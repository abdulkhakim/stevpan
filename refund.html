Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Our commitment to quality

Every STEVPAN pan is custom-manufactured to your exact specifications. We stand behind our workmanship and will address any issues promptly and fairly.

What is Eligible for Refund/Replacement

Manufacturing Defects
Cracks, warping, incorrect dimensions, or other defects caused by the manufacturing process. Full replacement or refund.
Wrong Specifications
Pan does not match the confirmed order specifications (size, thickness, corner radius, holes). Full replacement.
Damaged During Shipping
Pan arrived with shipping damage. We will file a courier claim and arrange a replacement.

What is NOT Eligible

Changed Your Mind
Custom products are made to your specifications. Cancellation is only possible before production starts.
Normal Wear & Tear
Steel pans naturally develop patina and minor surface changes with use. This is normal and not a defect.
Incorrect Order Placed
If you entered wrong specifications, we cannot remake it for free. You can place a new order at your cost.

How to Request

  • Contact us via WhatsApp or email within 7 days of receiving your order.
  • Provide your Order ID (e.g., SP-2025-0615-001).
  • Send clear photos showing the issue (multiple angles if possible).
  • Describe the problem briefly.

Resolution Timeline

  • 1-2 business days: We review your claim and may request additional information.
  • 1-3 business days: Claim approved or rejected with explanation.
  • 5-14 business days: Replacement manufactured and shipped, or refund processed to your original payment method.

Refund Method

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method used (FPX or credit/debit card via CHIP). Processing time depends on your bank, typically 3-7 business days to appear in your account.

Contact

  • WhatsApp: Use the chat button on our website
  • Email: support@stevpan.com
  • Include your Order ID in all communications for faster response.
